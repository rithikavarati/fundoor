# 🎉 FunDoor

Fundoor is an AI-native, decentralized activity discovery platform built with Caffeine AI and inspired by the challenge of finding fun experiences for families and kids.

Fundoor helps users discover things to do in different cities from a single place. Instead of visiting multiple websites, users can browse activities, view upcoming weekend events, save favorites, leave reviews, rate experiences, and quickly navigate to official activity websites for additional information or bookings.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🏙 City-Based Activity Discovery

Explore activities available across different cities through one unified interface.

### 📅 Weekend Activities

View activities happening over the next two weekends to make planning easier.

### ❤️ Favorites

Users can sign in and save activities they enjoy for quick access later.

### ⭐ Ratings & Reviews

Share experiences by rating activities and leaving reviews for other users.

### 🔗 Direct Website Navigation

Each activity card links directly to the official website for more information or reservations.

### 🧩 Category-Based Navigation

Quickly browse activities through dedicated sections, including:

* Theme Parks
* Museums
* Weekend Activities
* Favorites

## The Story Behind Fundoor

Fundoor was inspired by my own experience as a parent.

I often found myself searching across multiple websites to discover activities that would keep my kids entertained while also creating enjoyable experiences for the entire family. Planning weekends involved jumping between different sites, checking schedules, and trying to find options that suited everyone.

I wanted a simpler experience—a single place where families could quickly discover things to do, plan upcoming weekends, and save activities they love.

That idea became **Fundoor**.

---

## Why Fundoor?

Existing platforms such as TripAdvisor, Eventbrite, and Meetup provide valuable information, but Fundoor focuses on:

* Simplifying activity discovery.
* Helping families plan weekends more easily.
* Providing a city-first experience.
* Offering quick access to official activity websites.
* Delivering a personalized experience through favorites.
* Creating a community through ratings and reviews.
* Exploring decentralized application architecture.

---

## Built with AI-Native Development

Fundoor was created primarily through **AI-native development workflows and prompt engineering** using **Caffeine AI**.

Instead of manually writing every component from scratch, I used natural language prompts to iteratively design, refine, and build the application. This approach allowed me to focus on solving a real-world problem while accelerating development and experimenting with a new paradigm for software creation.

This project demonstrates how prompt-driven development can help rapidly transform ideas into working applications while allowing developers to spend more time on product design and user experience.

---

## Why Caffeine AI?

I intentionally chose Caffeine AI because I wanted to:

* Learn a new approach to software development.
* Explore decentralized technologies.
* Experiment with AI-assisted application development.
* Build on the Internet Computer Protocol (ICP) ecosystem.
* Try an alternative to traditional centralized platforms.
